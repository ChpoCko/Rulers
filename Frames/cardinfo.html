Rare

Rare cards are the most common cards, but they have a special place for every collector.

Epic

Epic cards are the second highest rarity available. Epic champions are a staple in every players deck.

Legendary

Legendary is the highest achievable rarity of a card. There is very limited amount of these cards

Tank

Tanks often take on an unofficial leadership role. They have high survivability and are able to sustain high amount of damage

Support

Supports are the backbone of the team. They provide utility for the whole team, which could change the outcome of the battle

Burst Dealer

Burst Dealers are the mercenaries of the team. They have the ability to deal a lot of damage quickly to confirm kills
